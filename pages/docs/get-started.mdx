# Quickstart Guide

> This Guide will help you get started with Lamatic.ai and quickly build and deploy your first project.

<Steps>

### Create Organization and Project

Setup  your first organisation and invite your team members to collaborate on projects.
![Signup](/public/signup-page.png)

Create your first project and start building your first flow.
![Proejct](/public/project-creation-page.png)

### Project Setup

Add Data Sources, Models, and Apps to your project to that  you will need for  your first flow.

![Home](/public/quickstart/project-home.png)

**Add model** - Review and select from a variety of AI models to find the best fit for your application.

![Models](/public/model-page.png)

Add Apps and Data Sources to your project to connect with your flows.
![Apps](/public/apps-page.png)

### Create Flows
    ![Workfows](/public/quickstart/workflow.png)
#### Flow Steps
    1. Drag Trigger node that will initiate the flow and connect it to the rest of the nodes.
    2. Drag other nodes to the canvas and connect them to create your first flow.
    3. Drag a response nodes that will send the output of the flow to the user.

### Test and Deploy
    Test your flow by adding a custom payload based on your schema.
    ![Test](/public/quickstart/test.png)
    When ready hit the deploy button. This will compile the flow and deploy it on the edge.

### Integrate
    See the API Docs to integrate your flow with your application.
