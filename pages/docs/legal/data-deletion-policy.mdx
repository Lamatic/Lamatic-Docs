# Data Deletion Policy

At Lamatic.ai, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users' data. This Data Deletion Policy outlines our procedures for the deletion of personal data collected through our platform.

## Data Collection
We collect various types of personal information, including but not limited to names, email addresses, and usage data, to enhance user experience and improve our services.

##  Purpose of Data Collection
The data we collect is used to:
1. Provide and maintain our services
1. Notify users about changes to our services
1. Allow users to participate in interactive features
1. Provide customer support
1. Gather analysis to improve our services

## Consent
By using our platform, you consent to the collection and use of your data in accordance with this policy.

## Exceptions to Deletion
Certain data may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes. If we cannot fulfill your deletion request, we will provide an explanation.

## Data Security
We employ robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

## Retention Period
Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by applicable laws. Once the retention period concludes, we will promptly delete or anonymize the data.

## Changes to the Policy
We reserve the right to modify this Data Deletion Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on our website. Regularly reviewing this policy is your responsibility to stay informed about how we protect your data.

## Contact Information
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Data Deletion Policy, please contact us at hello@lamatic.ai.
