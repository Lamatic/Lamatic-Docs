# PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated: 01 October 2024**

## 1. Introduction:

Welcome to Lamatic.ai, a website operated by Dinner Technologies, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our"). Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, Lamatic.ai.

## 2. Information We Collect:

We may collect personal information from you when you visit our website, interact with our services, or provide information for the purpose of placing a service order. The types of information we may collect include:

- **Personal Information:** Name, email address, contact details.
- **Service Order Information:** Information provided for placing service orders.
- **Device Information:** Browser, geographical location.

## 3. How We Use Your Information:

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- To process and fulfill service orders.
- To communicate with you regarding your service order or inquiries.
- To improve our website and services.
- To send promotional materials, with your consent.
- To comply with legal obligations, respond to enforcement requests, and protect our rights and interests in the event of claims or disputes.
- To evaluate or conduct a merger, divestiture, restructuring, reorganization, dissolution, or other sale or transfer of some or all of our assets, whether as a going concern or as part of bankruptcy, liquidation, or similar proceeding.

We will not collect, use, or share additional categories of your personal information for materially different, unrelated, or incompatible purposes without providing you notice.

## 4. Information Sharing and Processing:

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent. However, we may disclose your personal information under the following conditions:

- **Legal Obligations:** If required by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary.
- **Protection of Rights:** To protect and defend our rights or property.
- **Personal Safety:** To protect the personal safety of our users or the public.

### Legal Basis for Processing:

We are committed to minimizing the information we collect and limiting its use to where:

- We have your permission.
- Necessary to deliver our services.
- Required or permitted for legal compliance or another lawful purpose.

### Storage and Transfer of Information:

Your information is processed at our operating offices in the United States, and on servers provided by our vendors. If you utilize our services from a country outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to another country. Your information remains governed by this Privacy Policy to the extent permissible in each applicable jurisdiction.

### Retention of Your Information:

Your information will be processed and stored for as long as required to complete the purpose for which it has been collected. Personal information will be retained as long as needed for legitimate business interests or as required by law.

We secure your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. - Object to processing for specific purposes.
- Access and receive a copy of your information.
- Request restriction or deletion of your information.
- Receive a copy of your information in a machine-readable format.

You may request to exercise your rights by contacting us. We will endeavor to respond within 30 days from the receipt of your verifiable request. We cannot respond to requests if we cannot verify your identity or authority.
