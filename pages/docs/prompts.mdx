# Prompts

Prompts are the foundation of AI interactions in Lamatic. Use the Prompt IDE to create, test, and manage prompts that power your flows.

## Prompt IDE

The **Prompt IDE** enables you to craft custom prompts that align perfectly with your specific use cases. With **Prompt Templates**, you can design and fine-tune your prompts to elicit precise and relevant responses.

<Steps>
### Create Prompts
Use the Prompt IDE to write prompts with rich text formatting, variables, and role-based configurations.

### Test & Iterate
Test your prompts in real-time with syntax highlighting and preview capabilities.

### Reuse in Flows
Load saved prompts directly into LLM nodes in your flows for consistency and efficiency.

</Steps>
