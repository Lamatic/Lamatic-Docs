# Error Alerts & Notifications

Get notified the moment a deployed flow fails, by email and in-app, with a direct link to the failing log.

## What it is

Error alerts notify you as soon as a flow in a deployed project hits an error. Instead of keeping Logs open to watch for failures, Lamatic sends the alert to you directly.

## What it does

- **Email alerts**: includes the flow name, project, error message, and a "Check Log" link straight to the failing run.
- **In-app alerts**: same error details, surfaced in the notification bell and stacked as they come in.
- **Near real-time delivery**: alerts trigger per error within a 5-minute window.

## Managing your notifications

### Profile-level preferences

Your profile page has dedicated controls to turn email and in-app notifications on or off, independent of any specific project.

### Project-level settings

Each project has its own **Notifications** page under **Settings**. From here you can:

- Turn email and in-app alerts on or off for the whole project.
- Control which team members receive alerts, per recipient and per channel.

### Notification schedule (from the notification icon)

Open the **notification bell** in the navbar to set a delivery schedule for each notification preference. The schedule controls when external-channel notifications (like email) are delivered.

- Toggle specific days on or off, and set an allowed time range (**From** / **To**) for each day.
- Outside the scheduled window, external-channel notifications pause.
- **In-app and critical notifications are always delivered**, regardless of the schedule.

This lets you, for example, mute email alerts overnight or on weekends while still catching critical issues in-app.

This gives you full control over exactly who gets notified, how, and when, for every project.
