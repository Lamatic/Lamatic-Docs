## Core Platform Features

### üõ†Ô∏è Build
Tools to help you build AI applications and iterate faster.

- [Studio](/docs/studio) \- GUI for building and deploying AI applications.
- [Flows](/docs/flows) \- Build Agents, Process and Automations with custom flows using No Code and Low Code.
- [Tests](/docs/tests) \- Tools to test and debug your flow and application in development and before Deployments.
- [Context](/docs/context) \-Add Custom Context to your flows and Agents using VectorDB and memories.
- [IDE](/docs/ide) \- Integrated Development Environment for coding and debugging prompts, JS and API Requests.

### üß© Connect
Pre Built Models, Integrations and Widgets to help you connect with the best AI and Data sources.
- [Hub ‚Üó](https://hub.lamatic.ai) \- Perfect your flow with powerful apps, models and templates. Any flow is possible with a boost from the app marketplace..
- [Models](/docs/models) \- Find and Connect with the best model for your use Case.
- [Integrations](/docs/integrations) \- Integrate third-party data source and Apps into your flows .
- [Interface](/docs/interface) \- Embed AI Components like Search and Chat in your application with few lines of code.

### üöÄ Deploy
Monitor, Manage and Optimize your AI applications.

- [GraphQL API](/docs/interface/graphql) \- Integrate flow / Agents in your applicaiton with Federated and modular GraphQL API with automatically generated Docs.
- [Edge Deployments](/docs/deployments) \- Deploy and manage your agents and flows on the Edge.
- [Logs](/docs/logs) \- See real time request logs and traces.
- [Jobs](/docs/jobs) \- Monitor and Configure Schedule process.
- [Reports](/docs/reports) \- Analyze your projects with intuitive charts and queries.
